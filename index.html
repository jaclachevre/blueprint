Learn About the Commission Process

Sizing & Pricing

I offer a few canvas sizes, the most common are 5“x7”, 8”x10”. Prices range from $350 to $450. If you order 3-5 paintings, for perhaps family members or versions of one building, the price drops a bit and ranges from $275-$375 each.

How long does a commission take?

Having a full-time job outside of my painting, I move a bit slower than artists who have dedicated their lives to painting. Once I have received photos, I will give you a better estimate of how long it will take. On average, I give myself about a 2-3 months.

Do you offer digital copies of commissions?

I can produce digital files (jpeg, png or pdf files) and order art quality prints of any watercolor you commission. The digitizing process takes short while but for me it’s a fun one. This will be an additional charge.
